What is a whiteboard video?

A whiteboard video or sketch video simulates an artist drawing images in front of the viewer. They use simple, hand-drawn pictures, words, or charts to present information and ideas.

This style of storytelling is the most effective at breaking down difficult concepts. Then presenting that information so the viewer understands it. This makes it a great method for presenting and promoting your product online. Your audience can easily grasp what your business is all about and what you are trying to sell.
